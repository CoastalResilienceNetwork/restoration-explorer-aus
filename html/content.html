Port Phillip Bay Living Shoreline

The Restoration Explorer allows users to identify which nature-based technique(s) could work best in reducing coastal erosion, while promoting the multiple benefits of healthy coastal habitats. It currently provides guidance on four specific living shoreline techniques: Marsh and Mangrove Enhancement, Marsh and Mangrove Enhancement with Structures, Beach Enhancement, and Living Breakwaters. Definitions are provided within the Restoration Explorer.
